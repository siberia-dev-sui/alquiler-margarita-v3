Park Models

Compact, RV-class homes under 400 sq ft, designed for extended stays in RV parks or private land, offering a home-like feel in a small footprint.

Modular Homes

Homes built in factory sections and assembled on-site, offering faster build times and customizable layouts while meeting local building codes.

Tiny Home RV's

Compact, fully livable homes built on trailers for mobility and simplicity, typically under 400 sq ft and designed for full-time living or travel.

ADU's

Secondary homes built on the same lot as a primary residence, perfect for guests, rentals, or multigenerational living.

Manufactured Homes

Factory-built homes constructed on a permanent chassis, typically placed in mobile home parks or private lots and more affordable than site-built homes.

Container Homes

Modern, eco-friendly homes built from steel shipping containers, known for durability, quick setup, and industrial aesthetics.

Kit Homes

Pre-engineered home packages shipped with all necessary materials and instructions, allowing homeowners or contractors to assemble them on-site.

Garages

Structures primarily built to house vehicles but often designed with added lofts or apartments for extra living or rental space.

Custom Builds

Fully personalized homes designed and built from scratch to meet specific needs, preferences, and budgets.
